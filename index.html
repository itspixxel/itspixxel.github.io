Who I am

My name is Pixx

Based in Stoke-on-Trent, England, I am an aspiring game developer, self-taught artist, and storyteller. First-year student at Staffordshire University pursuing a BSc. (Hons) Computer Games Development degree. I primarily excel at Game Mechanics Programming but I'm well versed in 3D modelling and texturing, managing small and large-scale teams, time management, problem solving, and organization. I've worked on personal and student projects which have allowed me to gain experience in the games industry.

I love learning about Game Design, especially Environment Design! I love gaming and the communities it creates. I really enjoy working on long-term projects and often lead my group of friends creating small games together.

When I'm not developing or programming, I like to listen to lo-fi music, draw, and practice random things on Blender. I also occassionaly stream on Twitch playing games with my friends. I love horror and simulation games! I have a special place in my heart for the retro era and collecting things from that time period.

My Skills

Always learning, trying to broaden my horizons. Here's everything I've picked up on the way...

Game Engines

Equipped with the knowledge of Unity and Unreal Engine 5. I can easily navigate the engines and have a good knowledge of the pipelines.

Programming Languages

Experienced in C++ and C#. Good understanding of Object-Oriented-Programming, Garbage Collection, and Memory Management.

3D Modelling

Extensive experience in Blender. Primarily focused on Hard-Surface Modelling and creating 3D Assets for games. Some experience in Sculpting, Animation, and VFX.

Post-Production

6+ years of experience with Premiere-Pro. Experienced in editing in different styles and genres such as cinematics, documentaries, and product showcases. Keen sense of flow and great knowledge of creating custom FX. Some experience with Compositing, and Post-Processing.

Game Design and Art

Primarily focused on creating Open-World Environments with a good knowledge of real world weathering; wear and tear. Experienced in Stylized UI/UX Design. Some experience with level and interior design.

Real-World Skills

I have experience in working with and managing small and large-scale teams, working under deadlines, time management, organizational skills, problem solving skills, and a keen eye for perfection.
